06 — The Letter (Apology Page)
✦

Shazma,

I keep replaying it, and every time I do, I land in the same place — I was wrong, and I knew it even while I was arguing like I wasn't. That's the part I'm most sorry for. Not the disagreement itself, but making you sit in it alone while I defended something that was never worth defending.

You don't owe this site, this apology, or me anything. I built it anyway, because some things deserve more than a one-line "sorry" sent at 1AM. You deserve effort. You deserve to see that I noticed exactly what I did.

So — I win nothing if I win this. I'd rather lose every argument we ever have for the rest of our lives if it means you never feel small in one again.

Still yours, still sorry,
Adeeb
