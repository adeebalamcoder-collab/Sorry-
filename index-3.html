Shazma,

I've replayed that argument a dozen times and landed in the same place every time — you were right, and I knew it even while I kept talking like I wasn't. That's the actual problem here. Not the disagreement. The part where I let you sit there annoyed while I "made my case" for something that didn't even matter.

You don't owe this site, or me, anything. I built it anyway because a one-line "sorry" felt too easy for how wrong I was. So here's the harder version: I was wrong, you were right, and no amount of clever wording changes that.

You win. Officially. On the record. I'd rather concede this one cleanly than keep being "right" and have you stay annoyed at me over something this small.

Properly sorry this time,
